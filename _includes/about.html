About

I used to play drums in a totally awesome band and now I live out of a totally awesome van. While I was with The Begowatts I designed posters/stickers/t-shirts/whatever and eventually found that I actually kind of liked it. I know that being in a poor-ass band can be a ton of un-glamorous work and I started Jacurutu Productions to help these people out. I don't claim to be an expert in design, but if you like what you see above then you'll hopefully find my work adequate and my prices reasonable.
